### S11 — FAQ

**Status:** DRAFT

**Vertical-specific Qs:**

**Q:** What does the API integration look like?
**A:** REST API with SDKs for common stacks. Sandbox available on day one. Production onboarding 4–8 weeks depending on platform complexity.

**Q:** How is revenue share structured between CredX and the POS partner?
**A:** Per-platform agreement. Typical model: CredX captures the credit + servicing margin; the POS partner captures a share of the interchange-savings delta delivered to its merchants.

**Q:** Can we white-label CredX entirely under our brand?
**A:** Yes. White-label is the default for channel partnerships. Your merchants see your platform's brand, end to end.
