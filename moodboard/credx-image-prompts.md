Primary style direction:

- Premium B2B / B2B2B fairtech, not consumer fintech.
- Warm cream surface, dark navy line work, one Pink `#943F78` accent.
- Editorial, restrained, product-led.
- No Visa, MasterCard, BNPL, or real partner logos without approval.
- No generic stock-photo handshake imagery.
- No glossy AI realism, rainbow gradients, or neon app treatment.
